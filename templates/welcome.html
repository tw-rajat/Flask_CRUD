In the URL use (without quotes) the following syntax after "127.0.0.1:5000" to perform CRUD opertaion:

"/data/create" to Enter the elements.

"/data" to view all the elements prsesent.

"/data/id" to view particular element.

"/data/id/update" to Update the elements.

"/data/id/delete" to Delete an element.

"/uplaod" to Upload a file on the local server.
